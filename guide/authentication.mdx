## Overview

SAFI ControlHub uses a secure authentication system to protect your data and control access to devices. This guide covers account creation, login, profile management, and user roles.

## User Roles

ControlHub supports two user roles with different access levels:

- **Guardia (Guard)** - Standard user with monitoring access
- **Administrador (Administrator)** - Full access including device control and user management

<Note>
Your user role determines which features and devices you can access within the platform.
</Note>

## Creating an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    From the login page, click the registration link to access the sign-up form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Your Information">
    Complete the registration form with:
    - **Username** - Your display name (1-255 characters)
    - **Email** - A valid email address for your account
    - **Password** - Secure password (minimum 6 characters)
    - **User Type** - Select either Guardia or Administrador
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the "Register" button to create your account. You'll be automatically logged in upon successful registration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The password must be at least 6 characters long. Choose a strong password to protect your account.
</Warning>

### Validation Requirements

Your registration information must meet these criteria:

- **Name**: Required, 1-255 characters
- **Email**: Must be a valid email format and unique in the system
- **Password**: Minimum 6 characters, maximum 255 characters
- **User Type**: Must select either Guardia or Administrador

## Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Login Page">
    Navigate to the SAFI ControlHub login page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your registered email address and password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click "Log in" to access your dashboard. Upon successful authentication, you'll be redirected to the main device selection page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Login Errors

If you encounter login issues:

- **"El correo no esta registrado"** - The email is not registered in the system
- **"La contraseña es incorrecta"** - The password is incorrect

<Tip>
Your session remains active for 24 hours. After this period, you'll need to log in again.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Profile

Once logged in, you can view and manage your profile information.

### Viewing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile Page">
    Click on your profile icon or navigate to the Profile section from the main menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Information">
    Your profile displays:
    - Profile avatar (automatically generated from your email using Gravatar)
    - Username
    - Email address
    - User type (Guardia or Administrador)
    - Unique user ID
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Profile Avatar

SAFI ControlHub uses **Gravatar** to display your profile picture. To customize your avatar:

1. Create a free account at [Gravatar.com](https://gravatar.com)
2. Upload your preferred profile image
3. Link it to the email address used in ControlHub
4. Your custom avatar will appear automatically

<Note>
If you don't have a Gravatar account, a default avatar will be displayed.
</Note>

## Updating Account Information

Administrators can update user account information including username, email, password, and user role.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to User Management">
    Access the user management section (Administrator access required).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select User to Edit">
    Choose the user account you want to modify.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Information">
    Modify any of the following:
    - Username
    - Email address
    - Password
    - User role (Guardia/Administrador)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Submit the form to update the user account. A new authentication token will be generated automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Changing the email address requires the new email to be unique in the system. If the email is already registered, you'll receive an error: "El correo ya esta registrado".
</Warning>

## Logging Out

To securely end your session:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Logout Option">
    Click on the logout button in the navigation menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Logout">
    Your session will be terminated and authentication token cleared.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return to Login">
    You'll be redirected to the login page. All session data is removed from the browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Always log out when using shared or public computers to protect your account.
</Tip>

## Session Management

### Session Duration

- **Active Session**: 24 hours from login
- **Auto-logout**: You'll be automatically logged out after 24 hours of inactivity
- **Token Storage**: Session tokens are stored securely in browser cookies

### Security Features

- **Password Encryption**: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt rounds
- **Secure Cookies**: Authentication tokens use secure, SameSite=None cookies
- **Protected Routes**: Access to device controls and data requires valid authentication

<Note>
Your authentication token is automatically included in all API requests to secure your data access.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot log in with correct credentials**
- Clear your browser cookies and cache
- Ensure you're using the correct email format
- Verify CAPS LOCK is not enabled

**Session expires too quickly**
- Check your browser cookie settings
- Ensure cookies are enabled for the ControlHub domain
- Verify your system clock is set correctly

**Email already registered error**
- Each email can only be associated with one account
- Use a different email address or contact an administrator to recover your account

**Profile avatar not updating**
- Verify your Gravatar account uses the same email
- Clear browser cache to refresh the avatar image
- Allow up to 10 minutes for Gravatar updates to propagate

<Warning>
If you forget your password, contact your system administrator for assistance. Password reset functionality may vary depending on your deployment configuration.
</Warning>
